# SSFSSR Web Calculator
### Supply Side Food Self-Sufficiency Ratio Database (2010–2023)

A public web application providing country-level **Supply Side Food Self-Sufficiency Ratio (SSFSSR)** results for 188 countries from 2010 to 2023.

To the best of the knowledge of the Development Research Group, this is the **world's first public application** designed to calculate and present national food self-sufficiency ratios in a unified manner under common conditions.

## About SSFSSR

SSFSSR is an indicator developed to measure the extent to which each country can meet its domestic food supply through its own food production. This database was developed in accordance with the **10 Principles for Calculating Food Self-Sufficiency Ratios (10-PCFSSR)**.

## Methodology

This database is built on the **10-PCFSSR** (10 Principles for Calculating Food Self-Sufficiency Ratios). 64 commodities are classified into primary and secondary products and converted into a primary-product basis in caloric terms using **PPCR** (Primary Product Conversion Rate).

**References:**

- Goro Takahashi, *A New Method for Calculating the Food Self-Sufficiency Ratio: Supply-Side Food Self-Sufficiency Ratio*  
  Springer Nature · https://link.springer.com/article/10.1186/s40066-025-00570-z (65-commodity basis)

- *SSFSSR Version 1.1 — Formal Specification, PPCR Structural Coefficients, 64-Commodity Framework, and 2023 Global Dataset*  
  Zenodo · https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.18889852 (64-commodity basis)
